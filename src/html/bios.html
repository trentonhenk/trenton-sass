Meet the Band >;)

Harvey Dale

Harvey was born in the depths of Austin's most treacherous neighborhood...Westlake. He is an American country musician and the lead singer of Harvey Dale and the Cements.

Guy 'Fieri' Cement

This man's guitar skills will take your ears to flavortown. Melding funk, punk and electronic, Guy's beats will have your ears begging for more.

Reba Mcentire

Known for her solo career, Reba actually plays drums and performs back-up vocals for Harvey Dale and the Cements.
